# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and professional environment for everyone.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully;
- provide constructive feedback;
- share technical knowledge professionally;
- respect differing opinions;
- focus discussions on improving the documentation.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- harassment;
- personal attacks;
- offensive language;
- discrimination;
- spam;
- intentionally misleading technical information.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions that do not align with these principles.
